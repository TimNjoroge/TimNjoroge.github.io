SQL, Alteryx Project
Stock Market Analysis

50 Days Simple moving average is used by market analyst because historical analysis of price movements shows it to be an effective trend indicator. It is considered the most important because it's the first line of support in an uptrend or the first line of resistance in a downtrend. In this project I downloaded market data from finviz (https://finviz.com/). After clensing and transforming the data using alteryx, I uploaded to MsSQL and ran a script I wrote to extract data with the following parameters. Volume above 500K, Marker Cap above 2B, Past earning date and above 50 SMA
